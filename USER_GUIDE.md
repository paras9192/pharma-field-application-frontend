## 1. Logging In

1. Open the app and you will land on the **Login** page.
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password** provided by your admin.
3. Tap **Sign In**.

**Forgot your password?**
Contact your admin. They can send you a password reset link directly to your registered email, or set a new password for you from the system.

> **First time logging in?** If your admin sent you a "Set Password" link via email, click that link to create your password before signing in.

---

## 2. Dashboard

The first screen after login is your **Dashboard**. It gives you a snapshot of today and recent activity.

| What you see | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Greeting & date** | Confirms you are logged in and shows today's date |
| **Today's visits** | How many visits you have logged today |
| **Attendance status** | Whether you have checked in |
| **Recent activity** | Doctors, chemists, or visits added recently |

Admins and managers see a different dashboard with team-wide stats, pending dues, and attendance summaries.

## 3. Attendance — Check In & Check Out

The app records your presence each day through a simple check-in/check-out system.

### Checking In

1. Tap **Attendance** in the bottom navigation.
2. You will see today's status at the top.
3. Tap **Check In**.
4. The app will ask for your **location** — allow it so your check-in is geo-tagged.
5. A confirmation message appears and your check-in time is saved.

### Checking Out

1. Go back to **Attendance** at the end of your day.
2. Tap **Check Out**.
3. Your work hours for the day are automatically calculated.

### Viewing History

Switch to the **History** tab on the Attendance page to see your past check-in and check-out times.

> **Note:** You can only check in once per day. If you missed a check-in, contact your admin.

## 4. Doctors

This is where you manage the doctors you visit as part of your field work.

### Viewing Doctors

Tap **Doctors** in the bottom navigation. You will see a list of all doctors assigned to or added by you. Use the **search bar** at the top to find a specific doctor by name, phone, or specialisation.

### Adding a Doctor

1. Tap the **+ Add** button (top right).
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Name** — required
   - **Phone** — required
   - **Territory** — required (select from the list)
   - **Specialisation** — (e.g. Cardiologist, General Physician)
   - **Hospital / Clinic** — where they practice
   - **Birthday & Anniversary** — optional, useful for relationship management
3. Tap **Save**.

### Viewing a Doctor's Profile

Tap any doctor in the list to open their profile. Here you can:

- See all their contact details and territory
- View their birthday and anniversary dates
- Tap **Log Visit** to quickly record a visit with this doctor pre-filled
- Edit their profile (if you added them, or if you are an admin)

### Editing a Doctor

Open the doctor's profile and tap **Edit**. Make your changes and tap **Save**.

> You can only edit doctors you added yourself. Admins can edit anyone's records.

## 5. Chemists (Parties)

Chemists are the retail shops or pharmacies you supply to and collect payments from.

### Viewing Chemists

Tap **Chemists** in the bottom navigation. You will see all chemists available to you. Use the search bar to filter by name, owner, or area.

### Adding a Chemist

1. Tap **+ Add**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Shop Name** — required
   - **Owner Name** — required
   - **Phone** — required
   - **Territory** — required
   - **Address, Email, GST Number** — optional but recommended
3. Tap **Save**.

### Chemist Details Page

Open any chemist to see:

- Contact information and territory
- **Outstanding Due amount** — the total unpaid balance on their bills (visible to admins)
- **Photos** — you can upload photos of the shop or any documents
- **Log Visit** — record a field visit directly from here
- **Remind** button (admin only) — sends a payment reminder to the chemist if they have outstanding dues

### Uploading Photos

On the chemist detail page, tap **Add Photo**. You can select photos from your phone's gallery or take a new photo with your camera. Multiple photos can be uploaded at once.

## 6. Logging a Visit

Visits are the core of your daily field activity. Every doctor or chemist meeting should be logged as a visit.

### Creating a Visit

There are two ways:

**Option A — From the Doctors or Chemists page**
Open the doctor or chemist profile and tap **Log Visit**. The visit form opens with their details already filled in.

**Option B — From the Visits menu**
Tap **Visits** in navigation, then tap **+ New Visit**.

### Filling in the Visit Form

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Visit Type** | Doctor or Chemist |
| **Doctor / Chemist** | Select from the dropdown |
| **Territory** | Auto-fills based on selection |
| **Visit Date** | Today's date is pre-filled; change if needed |
| **Purpose** | Why you visited (e.g. product detailing, follow-up) |
| **Notes** | Anything you want to record about the meeting |
| **Products Discussed** | Add one or more products with quantity and details |
| **Follow-up Date** | Set a reminder date for the next visit |
| **Status** | Completed / Pending / Cancelled |

After filling in the form, tap **Save Visit**.

### Viewing Your Visits

The Visits list shows all your recorded visits. Tap any visit to see the full details. You can also edit a visit if you need to update it.

## 7. Bills

Bills track the invoices you have raised against chemists.

### Viewing Bills

Tap **Bills** in the navigation. You can filter bills by:
- **Status** — Pending, Partially Paid, Paid
- **Date range**

### Creating a Bill

Only admins can create bills.

1. Tap **+ New Bill**.
2. Select the **Chemist**.
3. Enter the **Bill Number** (original bill ID from your invoice).
4. Enter the **Total Amount**.
5. Set a **Due Date** (optional).
6. Add any **Notes** and tap **Save**.

### Bill Status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Full amount is unpaid |
| **Partially Paid** | Some amount has been received |
| **Paid** | Fully settled |

You can view payment history against each bill from the Bill Details page.

---

## 8. Payments

Payments records track money received from chemists against their outstanding bills.

### Viewing Payments

Tap **Payments** in the navigation. You will see a summary of total collected, total outstanding, and individual payment records. Filter by date range using the **From** and **To** fields at the top.

### Payment Modes

Payments can be recorded as:
- Cash
- Cheque
- UPI
- NEFT / Bank Transfer

## 9. Daily Reports

Daily reports are your end-of-day summary submitted to your manager.

### Creating a Daily Report

1. Tap **Daily Reports** and then **+ New Report**.
2. The date is pre-filled to today.
3. Fill in:
   - **Products Discussed** — which products you promoted today
   - **Highlights** — anything positive that happened
   - **Challenges** — difficulties you faced
   - **Competitor Activity** — any competitor info you observed
   - **Remarks** — any other notes
4. Tap **Save**. The report is saved as a **Draft**.

### Submitting a Report

Open a draft report and tap **Submit**. Once submitted, it goes to your manager for review and cannot be edited.

### Report Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | Saved but not yet submitted |
| **Submitted** | Sent to manager, awaiting review |
| **Approved** | Manager has approved it |
| **Rejected** | Needs correction — check the remarks |

## 10. Notifications

The bell icon in the top-right corner shows your in-app notifications.

- A **red badge** on the bell means you have unread notifications.
- Tap the bell to open the notification panel.
- Tap any notification to go to the relevant record (visit, bill, etc.).
- Tap **Mark all read** to clear all badges at once.

### Push Notifications

If your device and browser support it, you will receive push notifications even when the app is not open. Make sure to **Allow** notifications when the app asks for permission.

## 11. Your Profile & Settings

Tap the **Settings** icon at the bottom navigation.

### What you can do here

- **View your profile** — name, email, phone, role, and employee code
- **Change your password** — enter your current password, then your new one (min 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, and a number)
- **Sign out** — logs you out securely

## 12. What Can Each Role Do?

Different roles in the app have different levels of access.

| Feature | MR / Sales Person | ASM / ZSM | Admin | Super Admin |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| View Dashboard | Own stats | Own stats | Team stats | Full stats |
| Mark Attendance | Yes | Yes | Yes (view team) | Yes (view team) |
| Add Doctors | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Edit any Doctor | Own only | Own only | Yes | Yes |
| Add Chemists | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Edit any Chemist | Own only | Own only | Yes | Yes |
| View Outstanding Dues | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Send Payment Reminder | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Log Visits | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Create Bills | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| View Payments | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Submit Daily Reports | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Manage Team (Users) | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Manage Territories | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Assign Chemists to Reps | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Reset User Passwords | No | No | Yes (non-admin) | Yes (all) |
| Send Reset Link by Email | No | No | Yes | Yes |

## Quick Tips

- **Lost your place?** Tap the **Home** icon to go back to the Dashboard.
- **Slow loading?** The app works offline for cached data. Full features need an internet connection.
- **Something went wrong?** A red message will appear at the top. Try again or contact your admin if the error persists.
- **Session expired?** If you see a login screen after being active, your session timed out. Sign in again — your data is safe.
